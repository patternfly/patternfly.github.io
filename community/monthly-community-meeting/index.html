Monthly Community Meeting

Join Us

PatternFly has a diverse community, therefore we have organized smaller community meetings around our JS framework implementations, in addition to our larger PatternFly community meeting.

Agenda topics for community meetings include:

  • Coordination with community on various initiatives.
  • Updates on current status including demos of recent designs and/or implementations.
  • Capture community requirements and/or feedback.

Community meeting schedules and agendas are posted here and sent to the PatternFly mailing list. Smaller community meetings are scheduled dependent on the demand of that community with a cadence of 1-2 months. The larger community meeting occurs on a quarterly basis.

Community Meeting Leads:

  • PatternFly: Leslie Hinson
  • Angular PatternFly: Colleen Hart and Jeff Phillips
  • PatternFly ng: Cat Robson and Dana Gutride
  • PatternFly React: Jenn Giardino and Patrick Riley
  • Patternfly Web Components: Patrick Riley
